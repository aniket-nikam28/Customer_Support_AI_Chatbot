Store name : UrbanCart 
UrbanCart is a modern e-commerce platform offering a wide range of products – from the latest gadgets and electronics to fashion, home essentials, and lifestyle items. With fast delivery, secure payments, and 24/7 customer support, UrbanCart is designed to make online shopping simple, reliable, and enjoyable.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I create an account?
A: Click "Sign Up" on the homepage and enter your details. You’ll receive a confirmation email.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: After logging in, go to "My Orders" → select your order → click "Track".

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, and PayPal.

Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?
A: Yes, you can update the shipping address within 12 hours of placing the order from "My Account → Addresses".

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: You can email us at support@aniketstore.com or call +91-9876543210.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

Q: Can I delete my account?
A: Yes, please email support@aniketstore.com with your request, and our team will assist you.

Q: How do I cancel my order?
A: Go to "My Orders" → select your order → click "Cancel". Orders can only be canceled before shipping.

Q: What should I do if my order is delayed?
A: Check the "Track Order" page for updates. If delayed beyond the estimated delivery date, contact support.

Q: Do you offer same-day delivery?
A: Yes, same-day delivery is available in select cities for eligible products.

Q: Can I schedule delivery for a specific date?
A: Currently, scheduling is not supported. Orders are delivered as per courier timelines.

Q: Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?
A: Yes, COD is available for orders below ₹10,000.

Q: What should I do if my payment fails?
A: Please retry after checking your payment details. If the issue persists, use an alternative method or contact support.

Q: How do I return a product?
A: Go to "My Orders" → select your product → click "Return" and follow the instructions.

Q: How long does a refund take?
A: Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned item is received.

Q: Can I exchange a product?
A: Yes, exchanges are allowed within 7 days of delivery for eligible products.

Q: How do I check if a product is in stock?
A: Stock availability is displayed on the product page. If unavailable, you can choose "Notify Me".

Q: Do you offer product warranties?
A: Yes, warranty details are provided on the product page for eligible items.

Q: How do I apply a coupon code?
A: On the checkout page, enter your coupon code in the "Apply Coupon" box and click "Apply".

Q: Can I combine multiple discounts?
A: No, only one coupon code or discount can be applied per order.

Q: Do you have live chat support?
A: Yes, live chat is available 9 AM – 9 PM IST on our website and mobile app.

Q: What are your customer support hours?
A: Our support team is available 24/7 via email and from 9 AM – 9 PM IST via phone/chat.

Q: Do you deliver internationally?
A: Currently, we only ship within India.

Q: Can I preorder items?
A: Yes, if a product supports preordering, you will see the "Preorder" option on the product page.

Q: Do you provide gift wrapping?
A: Yes, gift wrapping is available for a small additional charge at checkout.

Q: How do I update my profile details?
A: Go to "My Account" → "Profile" and edit your details.

Q: Can I save multiple addresses?
A: Yes, you can add and manage multiple addresses under "My Account → Addresses".

Q: Do you have a mobile app?
A: Yes, download our app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

Q: How do I unsubscribe from marketing emails?
A: Click "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any marketing email.

Q: What should I do if I receive a damaged product?
A: Please raise a return request within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged item.

Q: Can I order without an account?
A: Yes, guest checkout is available, but creating an account helps track your orders easily.

Q: Do you offer bulk purchase discounts?
A: Yes, for bulk orders, please contact sales@aniketstore.com.

Q: Can I pay in installments?
A: Yes, EMI options are available for select credit cards at checkout.

Q: How do I know if my order is confirmed?
A: You will receive a confirmation email and SMS once your order is placed successfully.

Q: What happens if I miss my delivery?
A: The courier will attempt delivery up to 3 times. After that, the order will be returned to us.

Q: How can I change my phone number linked to the account?
A: Go to "My Account → Profile" and update your phone number.

Q: Do you charge for shipping?
A: Shipping is free for orders above ₹499. Below that, a small fee applies.

Q: How do I check my wallet balance?
A: Go to "My Account → Wallet" to view your balance.

Q: What should I do if I enter the wrong email address?
A: Contact support immediately to update your email.

Q: Can I reorder a past purchase?
A: Yes, go to "My Orders" and click "Reorder" for the desired product.

Q: How do I know if a product is eligible for return?
A: Eligibility is mentioned on the product page and during checkout.

Q: Do you offer loyalty rewards?
A: Yes, you earn reward points for every purchase, which can be redeemed later.

Q: Can I change the delivery pincode after placing an order?
A: No, you need to cancel and reorder with the correct address.

Q: Do you provide invoices for orders?
A: Yes, downloadable invoices are available under "My Orders".

Q: Can I update my payment method after placing an order?
A: No, once the order is placed, the payment method cannot be changed.

Q: How do I check current offers?
A: Visit the "Offers" section on our homepage or app.

Q: Do you provide installation services?
A: Yes, for appliances and electronics, free or paid installation is available.

Q: Can I split my order into multiple deliveries?
A: Yes, depending on product availability, items may be shipped separately.

Q: What should I do if I forget my registered email?
A: Contact support with your phone number and order details for assistance.

Q: Can I track my refund status?
A: Yes, refund details are available under "My Orders → Refund Status".
